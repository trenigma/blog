For some time now, I've batted around the idea of creating a blog. But of course analysis paralysis got in the way. What would I write about? Would it be interesting to anyone? Should I try to separate it by topics? Interests? And on and on. 

And then I finally decided, "just do it for me.” Let it be random, eclectic, sometimes silly. Let it reflect all of me. 

So here it is 🥰. Welcome to Views From the Treehouse 🏕️ 🌄. Grab your fav beverage, a snack, and let’s hang out. Thanks for coming 🫶🏽.
